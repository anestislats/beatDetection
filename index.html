Find out the beat of a song

Give the name of artist-song, or upload your track.

Beat Detector is a web application that calculates the tempo of any track.

You only need to know the name of the track or have stored the track to your computer.

Name a Track

Give the Name of Artist - Track and Detect the Beat .

The name of the track has to be in form "Artist's name - Song's name" (e.g. AC/DC - Ride on)

Upload Your Track

Upload a track from your computer and find out its beat.

The track has to be maximum 20mb and (.mp3), (.mp4) or (.wav) formated.

About

This project created in 2015, for the Ionian University. It constitutes academic work for the study of Speech and Sound Editing.

A user picks a track by two possible ways, (by name or by uploading) and the application calculates the BPM of the track with two different ways, (using a Javascript or a Matlab algorith.), one way for each case. Beat detector also shows the BPM of the track according to The Echo Nest.

The project uses the Echo Nest and the Spotify API.
